<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Pre Print Flexo Press market consists of three main types: Central Impression Flexo Press, Stack Flexo Press, and Inline Flexo Press. Central Impression Flexo Press utilizes a single cylinder to transfer ink and print onto a web of material as it passes through the center of the press. Stack Flexo Press operates with individual print stations stacked vertically, allowing for a wide variety of printing options. Inline Flexo Press integrates all the necessary components in a single line, maximizing efficiency and productivity. These market types cater to different printing requirements and offer diverse solutions.</p></p>
