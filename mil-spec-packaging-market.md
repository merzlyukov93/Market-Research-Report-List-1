Film Foil packaging is made of a combination of plastic and aluminum materials, providing a barrier against moisture, gases, and light. Poly Kraft packaging is constructed using laminated kraft paper coated with polyethylene, providing durability and protection against abrasion. Poly Mylar packaging is made of polyester film coated with aluminum, creating a barrier against moisture, gases, and light, while also providing puncture resistance. These packaging types are designed to ensure the safe and secure transportation of military goods.</p></p>
