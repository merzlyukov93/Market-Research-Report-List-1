<p><p>The thermoplastic polyester market is categorized into three main types: PET (polyethylene terephthalate), PBT (polybutylene terephthalate), and PETP (polyethylene terephthalate co-polymer). PET is widely used in the production of bottles, fibers, films, and packaging materials due to its excellent mechanical and chemical resistance properties. PBT is commonly utilized in electrical components, automotive parts, and industrial equipment due to its high strength and dimensional stability. PETP, a co-polymer of PET, offers enhanced heat resistance and is often employed in applications requiring superior mechanical and electrical properties.</p></p>
