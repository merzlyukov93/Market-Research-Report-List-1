<p><p>PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) Membrane Filter market consists of two main types: Oleophobic and Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane Filters. Oleophobic filters are designed to repel oils and are commonly used in applications where oil or hydrophobic substances need to be filtered out while allowing water or hydrophilic substances to pass through. On the other hand, hydrophobic filters are designed to repel water and are used in applications where water or hydrophilic substances need to be filtered out while allowing oils or hydrophobic substances to pass through. Both types cater to different filtration needs based on the substance being filtered.</p></p>
