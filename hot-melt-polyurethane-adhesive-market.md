<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The hot melt polyurethane adhesive market consists of two types: non-reactive hot melt adhesives and reactive hot melt adhesives. Non-reactive hot melt adhesives do not undergo any chemical reactions during the bonding process, relying solely on cooling to achieve their adhesive properties. On the other hand, reactive hot melt adhesives undergo a chemical reaction upon cooling, resulting in a stronger and more durable bond. Both types offer advantages in terms of ease of use, versatility, and bond strength, catering to various industries' adhesive needs.</p></p>
