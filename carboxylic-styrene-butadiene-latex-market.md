Emulsion Polymerization is a process where monomers are first dispersed in water and then polymerized under specific conditions, resulting in stable latex. This method is cost-effective and widely used in the CSBL market. On the other hand, Solution Polymerization involves dissolving monomers in solvents, followed by polymerization. It offers better control over polymer structure and properties, making it suitable for specialized applications in the CSBL market.</p></p>
