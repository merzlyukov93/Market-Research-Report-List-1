<p><p>Motorized positioners for robots are devices that allow robots to move and position in various directions. There are three types of motorized positioners available in the market: 2-axis, 1-axis, and multi-axis. </p><p>A 2-axis positioner enables a robot to move in two directions, typically up and down or left and right. This type of positioner is suitable for applications that require simple movements along two axes. </p><p>A 1-axis positioner allows a robot to move in a single direction, such as up and down or forward and backward. These positioners are useful for linear movements or applications that only require movement along one axis. </p><p>Lastly, a multi-axis positioner provides a robot with the ability to move and position in multiple directions simultaneously. This type of positioner is ideal for complex tasks that require precise and coordinated movements along multiple axes.</p></p>
