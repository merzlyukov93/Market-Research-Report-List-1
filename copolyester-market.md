<p><p>Copolyester refers to a type of polymer that combines two or more different ester monomers during its production process. Various types of copolyesters exist in the market. For instance, PET (Polyethylene terephthalate) is commonly used in packaging applications due to its excellent clarity and recyclability. PCTG (Polycyclohexane-1,4-dimethylterephthalate) offers high impact strength and is often utilized in medical and consumer goods. PCTA (Polycyclohexyl terephthalate) is known for its high heat resistance and is commonly employed in automotive and electronic applications. PETG (Polyethylene terephthalate glycol) is used in applications that require both toughness and clarity. PCT (Polycyclohexanedimethylene terephthalate) is a specialty copolyester with various properties suitable for specific applications in industries like automotive and electrical.</p></p>
