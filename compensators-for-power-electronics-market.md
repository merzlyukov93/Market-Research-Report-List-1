<p><p>Compensators for power electronics market types include the Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC), which regulates the transmission line voltage by injecting a controllable voltage; the Thyristor-Switched Series Capacitor (TSSC), which provides variable reactive power compensation by switching capacitors; the Thyristor-Switched Series Reactor (TSSR), which achieves voltage stability by switching reactors; the Thyristor-Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC), which adjusts the compensation level by controlling the thyristor-switched capacitor; and the Thyristor-Controlled Series Reactor (TCSR), which offers dynamic reactive power compensation by controlling the thyristor-switched reactors. These technologies enhance the stability and efficiency of power systems.</p></p>
