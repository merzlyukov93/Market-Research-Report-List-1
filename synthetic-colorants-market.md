<p><p>Synthetic colorants refer to artificial substances used to provide color to various products. The market types in this industry include pigments, dyes, masterbatches, color concentrates, and others. Pigments are insoluble particles that create color through absorption and reflection of light. Dyes, unlike pigments, are soluble substances that penetrate the material to create color. Masterbatches are color concentrates consisting of pigments or dyes, used to color plastic products during the manufacturing process. Color concentrates, on the other hand, are similar to masterbatches but are primarily used in the coloring of other materials like paints and inks. Other types may include specialty colorants or additives catering to specific applications.</p></p>
