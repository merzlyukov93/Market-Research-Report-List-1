<p><p>Borosilicate glass is categorized into three types based on its composition. Non-alkaline-earth borosilicate glass does not contain alkaline earth metals, making it highly resistant to chemicals and thermal shock. Alkaline-earth-containing borosilicate glasses possess improved thermal properties due to the inclusion of alkaline earth metals. High-borate borosilicate glasses substitute boron with borate, resulting in enhanced chemical resistance. These different types cater to various industries such as laboratories, pharmaceuticals, and electronics by offering unique properties to suit specific applications, ensuring durability and reliability in their respective contexts.</p></p>
