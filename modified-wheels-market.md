<p><p>The modified wheels market consists of three types: one-piece forging, two-piece forging, and three-piece forging. In one-piece forging, the entire wheel is made from a single piece of metal, making it strong and durable. Two-piece forging involves welding a center disc to the rim, offering increased customizability and varying sizes. Three-piece forging uses three separate pieces - the center disc, inner rim, and outer rim - allowing for extensive customization options, such as different finishes and color combinations. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
