<p><p>Chitosan edible packaging film is available in three crystal forms: α, β, and γ. The α crystal form offers good film-forming properties, but it has limited water vapor barrier capability. The β crystal form provides better water vapor barrier properties and mechanical strength. The γ crystal form exhibits excellent water vapor barrier characteristics but is less stable in comparison. These crystal forms offer various options for manufacturers to select based on the desired properties and applications of the chitosan edible packaging film.</p></p>
