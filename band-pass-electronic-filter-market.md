<p><p>A Band-pass Electronic Filter is a circuit that allows a specific range of frequencies to pass through while attenuating others. There are two main types of Band-pass Electronic Filters: Passive and Active. </p><p>Passive Electronic Filters use passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to create the filtering effect. They are simple and cost-effective but can cause signal loss.</p><p>Active Electronic Filters use active components like transistors or operational amplifiers in addition to passive components. They can amplify the signal and offer better control over frequency response but are more complex and expensive.</p></p>
