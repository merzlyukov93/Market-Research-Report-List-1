<p><p>PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes are a type of pipes used in plumbing and heating systems to prevent the diffusion of oxygen into the fluid. There are two main types of PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes in the market. The first one is Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H), which is produced using a single type of monomer and offers excellent dimensional stability and chemical resistance. The second type is Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R), which is produced using a combination of monomers and provides enhanced flexibility and impact resistance. Both types of pipes are effective in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of plumbing and heating systems.</p></p>
