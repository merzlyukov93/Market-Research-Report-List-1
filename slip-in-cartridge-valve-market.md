Block Valve: These valves stop or regulate the flow of hydraulic fluid.</p><p>2. Check Valve: Designed to allow fluid flow in one direction while preventing reverse flow.</p><p>3. Safety Valve: These valves protect the system by relieving excessive pressure.</p><p>4. Regulating Valve: Used to control and regulate the flow or pressure of hydraulic fluid.</p><p>5. Split-Flow Valve: Allows the hydraulic fluid to be divided and directed into two or more separate passages or circuits.</p></p>
